# FlamSanct — Brand Brief
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2026-04-05  
**Purpose:** Complete brand direction for logo, icon, and app UI design. Use this document when generating logo concepts or briefing a designer.

---

## Product Identity

**Name:** FLAM SANCT (two words)  
**Tagline:** Cast it on yourself. It stays on.  
**Secondary tagline:** Let your light shine. Too big for who?  
**Origin:** Ultima Online spell incantation for Reactive Armor. Flam = flame. Sanct = protection.  
**Type:** Daily practice platform — fitness, nutrition, Stoic reflection, brotherhood.  
**Audience:** Men who do hard things on purpose. F3 PAX. Pre-dawn crowd. Guys who show up.

---

## Brand Soul

FlamSanct is not a fitness app. It is a daily practice. The F3 workout is the anchor — the hardest thing you do first, so everything after gets easier. The flame is not a weapon. It is your light — the thing already inside you that discipline reveals. The shield does not attack. It protects the flame and holds the line for the man next to you.

**The flame is the hero. The shield is the vessel.**

**Brand feeling:** A worn leather journal. A campfire after a hard morning. A locker room where nobody needs to explain themselves. Something that feels like it existed before smartphones — earned, not designed.

**Brand keywords:** Earned · Brotherhood · Honest · Pre-dawn · Stoic · No shortcuts · The line holds · Let your light shine

---

## Brand Personality

**Feels like:** A field manual, not an app. Marcus Aurelius, not a motivational poster.  
**Sounds like:** "Four sessions this week. Average RPE 7.8. Sleep down to 6.1 hours. The work is there. The recovery isn't keeping pace."  
**Does not sound like:** "Amazing work this week! You're crushing it! Keep up the incredible momentum! 🔥"  
**Reference points:** F3 Nation · The Daily Stoic · Pre-dawn brotherhood · Ultima Online (insider easter egg only)

## Color System

### Primary Palette (Dark-first — app is dark mode primary)

| Name | Hex | Role |
|---|---|---|
| **Forge** | `#1C1C1A` | App background · deepest dark |
| **Charcoal** | `#2E2D2A` | Card surfaces · primary containers |
| **Ash** | `#3D3C38` | Elevated surfaces · modals |
| **Slate** | `#5C5A54` | Borders · dividers · muted UI |
| **Ember** | `#C0632A` | Primary accent · CTA buttons · the Flam reaction · key data |
| **Flame** | `#E07840` | Hover states · active states · secondary accent |
| **Parchment** | `#F0EDE6` | Primary text · headings on dark |
| **Dust** | `#9C9A94` | Secondary text · labels · muted copy |

### Color Meaning
- Ember/Flame = the flame. Used sparingly. Every instance of ember color means something — a PR, a reaction, a CTA, an anomaly alert.
- Charcoal/Forge = the background. Where the discipline happens.
- Parchment = the words. Text should feel warm, not clinical white.
- Dust = the metadata. Supporting information, not the main event.

## Typography

### Hierarchy

| Level | Typeface | Size | Weight | Color | Use |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Display | Georgia or similar strong serif | 32–40px | Bold (700) | Parchment | Phase names, hero moments |
| Heading | Georgia or similar strong serif | 22–28px | Bold (700) | Parchment | Screen titles, section headers |
| Body | Georgia or similar strong serif | 16px | Regular (400) | Parchment | AI feedback text, Stoic passages, reflections |
| Label | Clean sans-serif | 11–12px | Regular (400) | Dust | All caps, letterspaced · dates, metadata, categories |
| UI element | Clean sans-serif | 14px | Medium (500) | Parchment | Buttons, navigation, form labels |

### Typography Rules
- Serif carries the weight. Body copy in serif feels like something worth reading — not a push notification.
- Labels only in sans. The contrast between serif body and sans labels creates hierarchy without noise.
- All caps + letterspacing for label layer only. Never for headings or body.
- No font below 12px in UI.
- Wordmark: FLAM SANCT — all caps, strong serif, tight letterspacing, no ribbon, no banner.

### Suggested Typefaces
- **Serif (headings + body):** Georgia · Playfair Display · Libre Baskerville · EB Garamond
- **Sans (labels + UI):** Inter · DM Sans · IBM Plex Sans

## Logo & Icon Direction

### The Concept
A single clean flame centered inside a shield silhouette. The flame is the focal point. The shield is the vessel — it holds and protects the light. No sword. No banner. No ribbon. No wordmark inside the icon.

The flame should feel like it belongs inside the shield — tended, not threatening. Like a torch in a keep, not a weapon on a battlefield.

### Icon Specifications

**Shape:** Classic heater shield silhouette. Clean edges. Not overly ornate. The shield outline should be a simple, bold stroke — not filled, or lightly filled with the darkest charcoal.

**Flame:** Single organic flame. Centered vertically in the upper two-thirds of the shield. The flame tip may break slightly above the shield top — suggesting the light cannot be fully contained. Ember orange (#C0632A) at the base, transitioning to Flame (#E07840) at the tip. Illustrated style, not photorealistic. Should feel hand-drawn but clean.

**Background:** Forge (#1C1C1A) — near-black charcoal. The icon background is dark always.

**Shield outline:** Parchment (#F0EDE6) or warm off-white. Thin stroke, not filled. Or filled with Charcoal (#2E2D2A) with a Parchment stroke.

**Style:** Illustrated. Hand-drawn feel but not rough. Closer to a badge or a crest than a tech logo. Think field manual illustration, not SaaS icon.

**Must work at:** 1024×1024px (App Store), 512×512px, 180×180px (iPhone home screen), 60×60px (small). The flame and shield must read clearly at 60×60.

**What to avoid:**
- Swords, daggers, or weapons of any kind
- Banners, ribbons, or scrolls
- The wordmark inside the icon
- Gold/yellow — use ember orange, not gold
- Fantasy gaming aesthetic — this is Stoic, not RPG
- Gradients that won't survive small sizes

### Wordmark
- FLAM SANCT — two words, space between them
- Strong serif, all caps
- Letterspaced slightly (+50 to +100 tracking)
- Parchment (#F0EDE6) on dark backgrounds
- Forge (#1C1C1A) on light backgrounds
- Never inside the shield icon
- Sits below or to the right of the shield mark
- Full lockup: shield icon + wordmark below, centered

### Logo Variations Needed
1. **Icon only** — shield + flame, no wordmark (for app icon, favicon)
2. **Full lockup — dark** — icon + FLAM SANCT wordmark below, on Forge background
3. **Full lockup — light** — icon + wordmark, on Parchment or white background
4. **Horizontal lockup** — icon left + wordmark right, single line
5. **Monochrome** — single color version for embossing, merch, etc.

## App UI Direction

### Overall Feel
Dark. Warm. Dense with meaning, not cluttered. Every element earns its place. Nothing decorative for decoration's sake. Generous whitespace. Heavy typography.

### Screen Anatomy
- Background: Forge (#1C1C1A)
- Cards/surfaces: Charcoal (#2E2D2A) with hairline Slate borders (0.5px)
- Primary text: Parchment (#F0EDE6) in Georgia serif
- Secondary text: Dust (#9C9A94) in sans-serif
- Accent elements: Ember (#C0632A) — sparingly
- Dividers: Slate (#5C5A54) at 0.5px
- Corner radius: 12px on cards, 8px on smaller elements

### Key UI Moments
- **The Flam reaction** — ember orange shield-flame icon. The only "like" in the system.
- **The AI feedback card** — parchment text on charcoal surface, Georgia serif, no icons. Just the words.
- **The Stoic passage** — displayed in Georgia italic, slightly larger than body, with the attribution in Dust sans below.
- **The streak counter** — ember accent, prominent but not flashy.
- **PR badge** — small ember pill, "PR" in parchment. Appears on benchmark results.
- **Phase badge** — "The Grind" in Dust sans, all caps, letterspaced. Present but not loud.

### Navigation
- Bottom tab bar: dark, minimal, sans-serif labels in Dust, ember accent on active tab
- Tabs: Today · Progress · Community · Messages · (Chef — role-specific)

### Tone in UI Copy
- Screen titles: short, declarative, serif. "Today." "The line." "Your week."
- Empty states: honest, dry. "Nothing logged yet. You know what to do."
- Error states: factual. "Couldn't connect. Try again."
- Success states: understated. "Logged." "Done." "Saved."
